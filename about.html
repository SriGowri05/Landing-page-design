About Micro Information Technology Services (MITS)

Founded in 2023, MITS is a student-focused platform dedicated to connecting ambitious learners with meaningful internship opportunities. We collaborate closely with leading universities to foster career development and real-world experience.

Our Vision

To be the premier platform where students, educational institutions, and companies come together to create lasting professional relationships, drive innovation, and empower the workforce of tomorrow.

Our Mission

At MITS, our mission is to bridge the gap between students and industry by providing easy access to internship opportunities, facilitating professional growth, and fostering a supportive community of learners and mentors.

Our Values

  • Integrity: We uphold transparency and honesty in all interactions.
  • Excellence: We strive to provide high-quality opportunities and services.
  • Inclusivity: We celebrate diversity and welcome students from all backgrounds.
  • Collaboration: We believe in working together to create success for all.
  • Innovation: We embrace new technologies and ideas to improve the internship experience.
